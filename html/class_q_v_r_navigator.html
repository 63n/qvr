Detailed Description

Navigator in the virtual world.

A navigator allows moving your Virtual Reality display setup through the virtual world.

Imagine your VR setup is the cockpit of a UFO that moves through the virtual world. The pose of the UFO in the virtual world determines the pose of the cockpit windows. In QVR, that means the screen wall coordinates of a QVRWindow will be transformed with the navigator matrix.

A UFO crew member can move freely inside the cockpit. In QVR, that means a QVRObserver moves relative to a navigator.

A navigator is configured via QVRNavigatorConfig.
